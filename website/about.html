The Founder
Lee Dulcio
DevSecOps Engineer · Security Researcher · Tampa Bay, FL

I'm a DevSecOps engineer and security researcher with experience building and securing cloud infrastructure on GCP. I've spent years working in IT and cybersecurity — writing vulnerability analysis, managing infrastructure, and automating the things that shouldn't be manual.

I started GorillaC because I kept seeing the same thing: small businesses in the Tampa Bay area with outdated websites, no security posture, and no one to call. The technology to fix that exists — it just wasn't being made available to businesses that aren't Fortune 500.

GorillaC is my answer to that. Local, accountable, and technical — not a faceless agency that outsources everything.

Why GorillaC Exists

Most web agencies in this market charge enterprise prices, take 3 months, and hand you off to a project manager who doesn't know what Terraform is. Most IT consultants are focused on mid-market and above. Small businesses get the leftovers.

GorillaC is built around infrastructure-as-code and automation. Every client site we deploy is provisioned the same way, monitored the same way, and backed up the same way. There's no "we'll get to it" — it's built into the system.

We're based in Wesley Chapel and focused on the Pasco / Tampa Bay market. When something breaks, you're not calling a help desk in another time zone.
